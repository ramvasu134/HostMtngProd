4. Recording & Transcripts

Recording Consent

Teachers must obtain explicit consent before recording any meeting. Recording is disabled by default and must be explicitly enabled for each session.

Recording Usage

  • Recordings are stored securely on our servers
  • Only authorized users can access recordings
  • Teachers may download and archive recordings locally
  • Transcripts are auto-generated from audio content
  • Recordings must not be shared without consent

Data Retention

Recording data is retained according to institutional policy. Administrators may set automatic cleanup schedules. Users can manually delete recordings at any time.
